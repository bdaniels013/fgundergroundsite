About F&G Underground

Underground Utility Construction Done Right.

We're a heavy construction contractor specializing in the underground utility work that powers everyday life, from broadband fiber that connects neighborhoods to the water, sewer, gas, and electric lines that keep them running.

F&G Underground was founded on a simple principle: do skilled work, do it safely, and stand behind it. Whether we're operating as the Prime Contractor on a municipal utility installation or partnering as a Subcontractor on a regional fiber buildout, our crews bring the same standard of care to every bore, every trench, and every backfill.

We hold a Heavy Construction Contractors License, which means we can pull our own permits and self-perform the full scope of underground utility installation, keeping projects on schedule and accountability in one place.
