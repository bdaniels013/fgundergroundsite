Our Capabilities

Underground Expertise, End to End.

From the first locate to the final restoration, F&G Underground self-performs the full scope of underground utility installation, under any role, on any site.

How We Engage

Prime or Sub. We Operate Both Ways.

Bring us in to lead the project or to handle a critical scope. Either way, you get the same level of accountability and the same crews on the ground.

01 Role 1

Prime Contractor

We can lead your project from permitting through final restoration. With our Heavy Construction Contractors License we pull permits, manage subs, coordinate with utilities and inspectors, and deliver a single point of accountability for the entire underground scope.

02 Role 2

Subcontractor

Need a directional drilling or trenching specialist on your team? We partner with general contractors, ISPs, utility companies, and developers as a Subcontractor, bringing the equipment, crews, and underground expertise to keep your schedule moving.

Core Services

Directional Drilling & Trenching.

Our primary service is underground utility installation, performed by directional drill, by trench, or whatever the site demands.

Signature Capability
◎

Horizontal Directional Drilling

Precision HDD for crossings under roads, driveways, waterways, parking lots, and developed sites. The right method when surface disruption isn't an option.

◢

Open-Cut Trenching

Efficient trenching for greenfield development, subdivisions, and long utility runs: backfilled, compacted, and restored to spec.

⚛

Conduit & Vault Installation

Underground conduit pathways, handholes, vaults, and pedestals: set, sealed, and ready for cable pull.

⚙

Site Restoration

We leave the site as we found it, or better. Asphalt, concrete, sod, and grading restoration as part of the contracted scope.

⚑

Permitting & Coordination

With our Heavy Construction Contractors License we pull our own permits and coordinate directly with municipalities, utilities, and DOT, with no middleman and no delay.

Utility Types We Install

Fiber, Electric, Water, Sewer & Gas.

Underground installation across the five utility types that keep modern infrastructure running.

☷

Fiber Optic

Long-haul, middle-mile, and last-mile fiber installation for ISPs, municipal broadband, and enterprise networks. Bored or trenched, with conduit, splicing-ready handholes, and full path restoration.

⚡

Electric

Underground primary and secondary electric distribution: duct banks, conduit runs, transformer pads, and utility coordination for residential, commercial, and industrial sites.

♨

Water

Potable water main and service line installation in PVC, ductile iron, and HDPE, including taps, valves, hydrants, and connections to existing infrastructure.

♻

Sewer

Sanitary sewer mains, laterals, and force mains. Gravity and pressure systems installed to municipal and state DEQ specifications.

♽

Gas

Underground natural gas distribution lines and service installations using approved methods and materials, coordinated with the local utility.

Licensed & Permitted

F&G Underground holds a Heavy Construction Contractors License, which gives us the authority to pull our own permits, self-perform major underground utility work, and serve as the licensed contractor of record on Prime engagements.
